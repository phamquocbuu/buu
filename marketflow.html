Project Overview

The Marketflow SaaS Platform represents a comprehensive risk management solution designed to handle complex transaction processing, refund management, and customer blacklisting operations. As Senior Full Stack Developer and DevOps Engineer, I developed and maintained this critical platform that automates transaction management and data synchronization with banking systems.

This sophisticated platform integrates seamlessly with banking infrastructure while providing automated workflows for customer support processes through Gorgias helpdesk integration, ensuring secure and efficient handling of sensitive financial data and customer interactions.

Goals & Challenges

Project Goals

  • Develop a comprehensive risk management system for transaction handling, including refunds and customer blacklisting
  • Automate transaction management, data synchronization with banking systems, and customer support processes
  • Create secure and scalable infrastructure for critical financial operations
  • Integrate seamlessly with existing helpdesk systems for automated customer support

Key Challenges

  • Integrating and synchronizing transaction data securely with banking infrastructure
  • Automating workflows without compromising data integrity and security
  • Seamlessly integrating with Gorgias helpdesk system for automated customer support
  • Ensuring compliance with financial regulations and data protection standards
